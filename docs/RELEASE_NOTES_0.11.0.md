# Sustainable Catalyst Lab v0.11.0 — Mechanical and Thermal Engineering

## Scope

- 48 auditable methods
- statics and equilibrium
- stress, strain, beams, shafts, buckling, fatigue, fracture, fasteners, springs, gears, and bearings
- translational and rotational dynamics
- vibration screening
- incompressible internal and external flows
- ideal-gas and ideal-cycle thermodynamics
- conduction, convection, radiation, heat exchangers, and lumped transients
- 16 deterministic browser benchmarks
- browser/Python method parity from one catalog
- project, notebook, visualization, report, restore, and Decision Studio compatible records
- focused shortcode: `[sc_lab_mechanical_thermal]`

## Boundary

These are transparent calculation and screening methods. They do not replace finite-element, CFD, pressure-vessel, rotating-equipment, fire, HVAC, manufacturing, safety, code-compliance, or licensed professional review.
