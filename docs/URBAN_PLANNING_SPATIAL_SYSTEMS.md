# Urban Planning and Spatial Systems

The v0.14.0 workspace connects land-use analysis, accessibility, transportation, network structure, GIS indicators, infrastructure and public-service capacity, environmental exposure, equity, resilience, and scenario comparison in one traceable calculation environment.

Every calculation record preserves:

- method and release identifiers
- equations and assumptions
- labeled numerical inputs and units
- labeled outputs and units
- screening warnings
- validation-suite identifiers
- timestamps and audit fingerprints
- project and notebook handoffs
- visualization events

The spatial indicators are intentionally transparent. They are designed for comparative reasoning, early-stage analysis, teaching, documentation, and reproducible scenario work rather than black-box optimization or automated planning decisions.
