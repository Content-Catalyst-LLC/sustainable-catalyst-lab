# Aerospace Engineering and Flight Systems

The v0.18.0 workspace connects atmospheric properties, aerodynamic forces, aircraft performance, flight dynamics, stability and control, propulsion integration, structural loads, aeroelastic screening, navigation, mission accounting, and flight-system reliability in one auditable calculation environment.

Every analysis preserves:

- method and release identifiers
- human-readable equations
- executable output expressions
- labeled numerical inputs and units
- labeled outputs and units
- assumptions and screening warnings
- deterministic benchmark identifiers
- timestamps and audit metadata
- project, notebook, and visualization handoffs

The workspace is intentionally transparent and screening-oriented. It does not perform opaque aircraft optimization, autonomous flight-control design, certification analysis, or operational dispatch decisions.
