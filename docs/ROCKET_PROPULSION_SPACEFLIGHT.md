# Rocket Propulsion and Spaceflight

The v0.19.0 workspace connects propulsion performance, nozzle flow, launch-vehicle mass and staging, ascent losses, orbital mechanics, spacecraft power and thermal systems, communications, mission delta-v budgets, and reliability in one auditable calculation environment.

Every analysis preserves method and release identifiers, human-readable equations, executable expressions, labeled inputs and outputs with units, assumptions, screening warnings, deterministic benchmark identifiers, timestamps, audit metadata, and project or notebook handoffs.

The workspace is deliberately educational and preliminary-design oriented. It does not provide detailed combustion-instability, turbopump, propellant-chemistry, guidance-software, range-safety, launch-operations, weapons, or targeting design capabilities.
