# Research Quality and Method Review v0.29.1

The method-review registry turns quality assurance into project data rather than an informal checklist. Reviews can reference benchmark records, validation evidence, sources, reproducible runs, and provenance records already stored by the Lab.

## Review tiers

- **Exploratory:** lightweight review for early analysis and prototypes.
- **Standard:** requires benchmark coverage, validation evidence, limitations, reproducibility, and provenance.
- **High consequence:** adds stronger benchmark/evidence thresholds and an independent reviewer requirement.

## Governance history

Reviewer decisions are stored separately from the review record and appended to its history. Deprecation creates a dedicated project record so older reports and runs can retain the method identifier while the active registry clearly marks the method as deprecated.
