# Earth, Climate, Ocean, and Marine Systems Methods

Sustainable Catalyst Lab v0.8.0 added 83 browser-based methods grouped into solid Earth, atmosphere, climate, hydrology, ocean, marine systems, remote sensing, hazards, and carbon-cycle work areas.

## Solid Earth

Plate-motion displacement; seismic moment; moment magnitude; earthquake energy; geothermal gradient; lithostatic pressure; Airy isostatic root.

## Atmosphere

Isothermal barometric pressure; hypsometric thickness; dry-adiabatic parcel temperature; saturation vapor pressure; relative humidity; dew point; potential temperature; wind-power density; logarithmic CO₂ radiative forcing; atmospheric scale height.

## Climate

Climate anomaly; ordinary-least-squares trend; heating degree days; cooling degree days; forcing-scaled equilibrium warming; planetary equilibrium temperature; carbon-budget duration under geometric emissions change; aridity index; thermal and haline steric sea-level change.

## Hydrology

Catchment water balance; rational-method runoff; Manning open-channel discharge; Darcy groundwater flow; aquifer storage; Horton infiltration; Gumbel return level; suspended-sediment discharge; reservoir residence time; groundwater seepage velocity; soil-water storage.

## Oceanography

## Marine systems

## Remote sensing

NDVI; NDWI; NBR; EVI; weighted broadband albedo; thermal brightness temperature; top-of-atmosphere reflectance; confusion-matrix metrics; mapped pixel area; digital-number calibration; emissivity-corrected surface temperature.

## Hazards

Recurrence probability; infinite-slope factor of safety; Stokes ash settling; wind-driven surge setup; empirical coastal runup; contaminant advection-dispersion scale; uniform-film spill area.

## Carbon cycle

Atmospheric CO₂ mass conversion; emissions-to-ppm conversion; ecosystem carbon stock; sequestration total; soil organic carbon stock; methane CO₂-equivalent; ocean carbon flux.

## Validation

Twelve deterministic cases cover plate motion, seismic moment, humidity, CO₂ forcing, runoff, deep-water waves, tsunami travel, diversity, NDVI, recurrence probability, atmospheric carbon conversion, and soil carbon stock.

All browser methods expose their equation, input assumptions, result, warning state, and project-record destination. Screening methods explicitly retain limitations in their validation envelope.
