# Circular Economy and Industrial Ecology

The v0.16.0 workspace treats the circular economy as a measurable systems domain rather than a collection of slogans. It connects material-flow accounting, circular product design, repair and reuse, waste prevention, recovery infrastructure, industrial symbiosis, lifecycle impacts, resource productivity, supply resilience, and investment scenarios.

Each analysis preserves:

- method and release identifiers
- human-readable equations
- executable output expressions
- labeled numerical inputs and units
- labeled outputs and units
- assumptions and screening warnings
- validation-suite identifiers
- timestamps and audit metadata
- project, notebook, and visualization handoffs

The calculations remain transparent and reproducible. They do not perform opaque optimization or automate policy, procurement, investment, or regulatory decisions.
