# Connected Scientific Research Platform Beta — v0.40.0

Sustainable Catalyst Lab v0.40.0 unifies the research lifecycle, institutional governance, cross-product handoffs, public integrations, security, recovery, and performance evidence into a controlled institutional beta platform.

## Beta operating layer

- Institutional beta cohorts and onboarding journeys.
- Guided research projects spanning definition, sourcing, design, execution, analysis, review, publication, handoff, monitoring, and completion.
- Privacy-minimized, explicit-opt-in telemetry that hashes actor and workspace identifiers and never stores research payloads.
- Feedback, known-limitations, and support-case operations.
- A component-aware release-readiness report that blocks expansion when critical issues remain unresolved.
- Hash-chained beta-operation events.

## Boundary

This is a controlled beta. It does not claim general availability, does not bypass existing governance or security controls, does not store secrets or raw restricted data in beta records, and does not use telemetry without explicit opt-in.
