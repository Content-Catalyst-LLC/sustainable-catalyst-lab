# Sustainable Cities and Urban Resilience

The v0.15.0 workspace connects urban metabolism, resource flows, decarbonization, climate hazards, adaptation, critical infrastructure continuity, equity, social resilience, investment, governance, and integrated city scenarios in one auditable calculation environment.

Each record preserves:

- method and release identifiers
- a named planning or engineering formula
- executable output expressions
- labeled inputs and units
- labeled outputs and units
- assumptions and screening warnings
- validation-suite identifiers
- timestamps and audit fingerprints
- project, notebook, and visualization handoffs

The methods remain transparent by design. No opaque optimization or automated allocation decision is performed.
