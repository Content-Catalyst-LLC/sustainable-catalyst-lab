# Microbiology Laboratory

The v0.20.0 workspace connects microbial growth, continuous culture, enumeration, microscopy, environmental microbiology, antimicrobial and disinfection screening, microbial ecology, molecular-assay calculations, and laboratory quality control in one auditable environment.

Every analysis preserves method and release identifiers, human-readable equations, executable expressions, labeled inputs and outputs with units, assumptions, screening warnings, benchmark identifiers, timestamps, audit metadata, and project or notebook handoffs.

The workspace is computational and documentation oriented. It does not provide organism-cultivation instructions, pathogen enhancement, clinical diagnosis, treatment recommendations, or automated biosafety decisions.
