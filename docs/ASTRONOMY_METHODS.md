# Astronomy and Astrophysics Methods

Sustainable Catalyst Lab v0.6.0 adds a browser-based Astronomy and Astrophysics Laboratory with methods for celestial coordinates and time, orbital mechanics, planetary systems, stellar astrophysics, photometry, spectroscopy, galaxies, cosmology, and telescope imaging.

## Scientific boundaries

The methods use explicit simplified equations and preserve inputs, outputs, assumptions, validation warnings, and project context. They do not replace authoritative ephemerides, barycentric time conversion, full N-body integration, calibrated reduction pipelines, instrument response models, stellar atmosphere grids, general-relativistic ray tracing, or precision cosmology software.

## Validation

Ten deterministic reference cases cover Julian dates, parallax, Keplerian periods, solar luminosity, Wien peak wavelength, magnitude ratios, Hubble distance, diffraction, transit depth, and Schwarzschild radius.
