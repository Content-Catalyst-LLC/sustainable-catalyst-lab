# Mechanical and Thermal Engineering Laboratory

Sustainable Catalyst Lab v0.11.0 adds a shared browser/Python calculation environment for transparent preliminary engineering analysis.

## Method groups

- Statics and equilibrium
- Stress, strain, beams, shafts, and elastic stability
- Fatigue, fracture, factors of safety, bolts, springs, gears, and bearings
- Translational and rotational dynamics
- Single-degree-of-freedom vibration screening
- Incompressible continuity, Bernoulli, Reynolds number, pipe losses, pumps, drag, and orifices
- Ideal-gas and ideal-cycle thermodynamics
- Conduction, convection, radiation, heat exchangers, and lumped transient response

## Analysis contract

Each result records its method identifier, release and method version, equation, inputs, units, outputs, output units, assumptions, warnings, validation state, runtime, timestamp, and fingerprint.

## Project integration

Mechanical and thermal results can be saved to the active project, added to the notebook, sent to the shared visualization contract, included in Report Composer, exported with workspace packages, restored through validated data management, and passed into Decision Studio.

## Responsible-use boundary

These methods are calculation and screening tools. They do not replace detailed finite-element or CFD models, test programs, material certification, code compliance, pressure and rotating-equipment analysis, fire and life-safety review, HVAC design, manufacturing qualification, or licensed professional judgment.
